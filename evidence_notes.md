# Evidence Notes

This document summarizes the external references used to support the SkillPulse concept. These are not direct training datasets for the app. They are supporting evidence for the product narrative, skill-shift framing, and career guidance rationale.

## 1. ModernAnalyst: 10 Trends BAs Can't Ignore in 2026

**What it supports**

- agentic workflow design
- decision rights and governance
- monitoring, drift, and provenance
- the idea that modern business roles increasingly require technical and AI literacy

**Why it matters to SkillPulse**

It supports the argument that role expectations evolve rapidly and that new micro-skills emerge faster than static learning paths.

## 2. Cornerstone Skills Graph

**What it supports**

- skills graph / skills taxonomy thinking
- matching people, roles, and learning paths through a structured skills engine
- continuous skill-gap analysis and personalized career navigation

**Why it matters to SkillPulse**

It validates the broader product direction of building a skill-to-role mapping engine with personalized upskilling outputs.

## 3. Hays 2025 Skills Report

**What it supports**

- skills shortages
- fast-changing employer expectations
- the need for continuous upskilling and workforce adaptability

**Why it matters to SkillPulse**

It strengthens the business case that employability is increasingly tied to dynamic skill alignment rather than static qualification.

## 4. Additional AI / agentic workflow references

Your attached PDFs on agentic workflows, AI portfolio managers, job-finding agents, and AI analyst demand support the broader trend that:

- AI-assisted workflows are becoming mainstream
- applied AI skills are rising faster than purely academic model-building
- candidates benefit from demonstrable, workflow-oriented portfolio artifacts

## How to talk about evidence during judging

Use this line:

`Our personas are representative, but the underlying product rationale is backed by published material on skills graphs, agentic workflows, evolving analyst expectations, and continuous reskilling.`

## Important honesty note

Do not claim that the PDFs are the same thing as your live market dataset.

Say this instead:

- the app uses a curated or uploaded job dataset for analysis
- the PDFs are supporting references for the product framing and trend interpretation
